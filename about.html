How It Started

From a single truck to a three-city operation.

In 1969, Jim Logan Sr. bought his first service truck and hung a shingle on Little York Road in Dayton, Ohio. His promise was simple: fix it right, charge fair, and treat every home like it's his mother's.

Fifty-five years later, that promise still drives everything we do. Jim's son, Mike Logan, took over the business in 1995 and expanded to Columbus, then Cincinnati — but the culture hasn't changed. Our technicians aren't just employees. Many have been with us for 10, 15, even 20+ years. They know your neighborhood, your climate, and your equipment.

Today, the third generation of Logans is learning the trade — sweeping warehouse floors in the summer, riding along on calls, and earning their NATE certifications. We're building for the next 55 years.

Mike Logan
President & Second-Generation Owner
Our Journey

55 years of milestones.

1969

The First Truck

Jim Logan Sr. founds Logan Services with a single truck on Little York Road, Dayton. Heating repair and sheet metal ductwork are the bread and butter.

1985

Air Conditioning Era

Logan adds central A/C installation to its services and becomes a Trane Comfort Specialist dealer — a partnership that continues to this day.

2001

Columbus Expansion

Mike Logan opens the second location on Groves Rd in Columbus, bringing Logan's same-day service to central Ohio.

2012

Cincinnati / NKY

The third location opens on Beechmont Ave in Cincinnati, serving southwest Ohio and Northern Kentucky. Plumbing services launch the same year.

2024

#1 Volume Trane Dealer

Logan Services is named the #1 volume Trane dealer in the region. The company surpasses 50,000 homes served and 6,000 five-star reviews.
