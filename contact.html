FAQ

Before you call.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call?
For emergency calls (no heat in winter, no A/C in extreme heat, burst pipes, gas leaks), we dispatch a technician within 2 hours in most cases. Our emergency line is staffed 24/7 — call (800) 564-2611 anytime.
What information should I have ready when I call?
It helps to have: your address and ZIP code, a brief description of the issue, the brand and approximate age of your equipment (if known), and whether this is an emergency. But don't worry — our dispatchers will walk you through everything.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes. In-home estimates for new system installations (A/C, furnace, heat pump, water heater) are always free. For repair diagnostics, there's a $79 flat service call fee that includes inspection and a written quote.
Can I book online or do I need to call?
Both! You can fill out the form on this page and we'll call you back within one business hour to confirm a time. Or call (800) 564-2611 to speak with a dispatcher immediately. For emergencies, always call — we answer 24/7.
