Family-Owned & Locally Trusted

Three generations of Logan family service.

Founded in 1969, Logan Services began as a small HVAC shop in Dayton, Ohio. What started with one truck and a commitment to honest work has grown into the region's most trusted home comfort company — still family-owned, still answering the phone, still showing up on time.

Today, the third generation of Logans leads a team of 200+ certified technicians serving Dayton, Columbus, Cincinnati, and Northern Kentucky. We operate our own in-house training center, are the nation's #1 volume residential Trane dealer, and have earned the trust of more than 50,000 Ohio Valley families.

We don't just fix furnaces and clear drains. We show up as neighbors — because that's what we are.
