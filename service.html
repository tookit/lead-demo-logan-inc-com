FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do you offer same-day service?
Yes. For most service calls booked before 2 PM, we can dispatch a technician the same day. Emergency calls are handled 24/7 — just call (800) 564-2611.
How much does a diagnostic visit cost?
Our standard diagnostic visit is $79 flat. This includes a full inspection by a NATE-certified technician and a written estimate for any recommended repairs. No hidden fees.
Do you offer financing for new systems?
Yes. We offer 0% APR financing for up to 60 months on qualifying Trane system installations, subject to credit approval. We also have flexible payment plans for plumbing and other services.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the greater Dayton, Columbus, and Cincinnati metro areas, including Northern Kentucky. See our contact page for specific service zones and phone numbers by location.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Every Logan technician is NATE-certified, EPA-licensed, background-checked, and fully insured. We carry $2M general liability and workers' comp on every employee.
Do you offer maintenance plans?
Yes. Our Logan Comfort Club includes bi-annual tune-ups (spring + fall), priority scheduling, 15% discount on all repairs, and no overtime charges. Plans start at $14.95/month.

HVAC or plumbing emergency?

We're available 24/7. Don't wait — call now and we'll dispatch a technician right away.
